Engaging with the public is a vital part of science. Below are selected outreach activities where I shared astronomy and physics with broader audiences.

Magnet ICE (2021–2023)

An educational program addressing school segregation in Catalonia, Spain. I supported primary school teachers at Gabriel Castellà i Raich (Igualada) by co-designing and guiding hands-on science activities tailored to underrepresented students. This long-term engagement aimed to foster inclusion and curiosity through space-themed experiments and storytelling.
Project Link

Outreach Talk — Feb 2022

Celebrating the International Day of Women and Girls in Science, I gave a talk at the Institute of Space Sciences (ICE, Barcelona) to inspire high school students about careers in astrophysics.
Event Summary

Astronomical Observation — Nov 2022

Helped organize a public stargazing night at the Autonomous University of Barcelona, in collaboration with the Institute of Space Sciences. The event attracted a wide audience and included telescope sessions and guided explanations.
